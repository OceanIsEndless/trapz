About Game

Welcome to the world of Trapz.io! A hardcore online game where thrills, excitement, traps and dangers await you at every stage. Battle other players and become the strongest of them all!

Trapz.io is a multiplayer platform in the visual style of Trap Adventure 2. It is tagged the most difficult game ever, the hardcore Mario, the thrill, the insane adventure, but it leaves everyone filled with total excitement!

Welcome to a world where danger awaits at every step: unexpected spikes, disappearing platforms, falling ceilings, fire bullets and dangerous enemies. Jump, run, attack other players, collect coins and hearts. Overflowing emotions, the crazy thrill of the battle and that itch to get ahead will accompany you at all times!

You are a tiny human descending into the dangerous dungeon! Each game character begins with 100% health but for every wrong step, 100% turns to zero and the player will need to start from the beginning (return to the initial position).

You need to jump over traps and avoid enemies by evading their hits. Level by level you improve your skills and start feeling the danger coming far in advance the next time it happens.

The more progress you make, the more coins you encounter and collect! To take the treasure, all you need to do is merely touch it or run over it. Want even more coins? Then you will have to attack and kill other players! Opponent slain � all of his coins are yours! The stronger your attacks against your opponent, the more money you get. To restore your health, you need to find a heart somewhere on the level and run over it.

Do you have what it takes to try and defeat your friends? The rules of the Trapz.io online game are simple: do not let your guard down, defeat your enemy in n instant and finally, try not to die! Even if victory seems to be just one step away, theres always that one thing that can destroy you.

In this game, you cannot afford to get too comfortable because obstacles and dangers await you at every step. Only the bravest, smartest and most agile will be able to complete the game!

Will you turn out to be worthy of the winners title? Test yourself at home, work, school, in your spare time and on vacation!
